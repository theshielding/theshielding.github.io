Take Part #

Are you, or have you been, shielding? Or maybe you know someone who shields? If so, we would love to hear from you! Anonymous stories are welcome.

Fill out the form below, and we’ll be in touch.






You are of course also welcome to contact us directly: theshielding@protonmail.com.

How does it work? #

Once we’ve received your note:

  1. Together we’ll pick a time and format for a conversation (a.k.a. ‘interview’). This can be a (video)call or over email—whatever you’re most comfortable with.
  2. Your text will be transcribed (if applicable), edited and typeset. You’ll get a chance to read the complete text and add any corrections or clarifications.

I want to take part, but I don’t like calling #

That’s fine! We’re totally happy to do any interview in a text-only format, via email.

Or, alternatively, you could also initially write things down yourself, if that’s more your thing? For which you can use the available worksheet.
