Take Part #

Are you, or have you been, shielding? Or maybe you know someone who shields? If so, we would love to hear from you! Anonymous stories are welcome. Get in touch: theshielding@protonmail.com.

How does it work? #

Once we’ve received your note:

  1. Together we’ll pick a time and format for a conversation (a.k.a. ‘interview’). This can be a (video) call, email, messages or chat—whatever you’re most comfortable with.
  2. Your text will be transcribed (if applicable), edited and typeset. You’ll get a chance to read the complete text and add any corrections or clarifications.

I want to take part, but I don’t like calling #

That’s fine! We’re totally happy to do any interview in a text-only format, for example email, messages or chat.

Or, alternatively, you could also initially write things down yourself, if that’s more your thing? For which you can use the available worksheet.
