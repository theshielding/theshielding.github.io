The Shielding #

The Shielding is an initiative to gather the personal stories and experiences of those who are, or have been, shielding during the ongoing coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.

Why are you doing this? #

Our reason for doing this is to foster a sense of community and moral support. To let each other—fellow humans who are also shielding—know that we are not alone. The outside world may have fallen largely into pandemic denialism. But there are others out there who also shield.

These are their stories.

We are also doing this with an eye on the future. Because if we donâ€™t document our experiences ourselves, who is going to believe that it all really happened this way?

Can I take part? #

Of course! We are currently collecting shielding stories. Anonymous stories are welcome. Are you, or have you been, shielding? Or do know someone who is (or has been)? Please do get in touch! We’d love to hear from you: theshielding@protonmail.com.

If you’d prefer to write things down yourself, you’re welcome to used the available worksheet.
